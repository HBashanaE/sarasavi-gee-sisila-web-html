About Us

The University of Moratuwa is Sri Lanka's top state university for technology, offering programs in engineering, architecture, ICT, business, and medicine. It supports the nation's youth in higher education and career advancement. Graduates from the university are highly sought after by industry and employers, with hundreds of thousands employed at convocation.

The Department of Building Economics at the University of Moratuwa, founded in 1983, is a pioneer in undergraduate Quantity Surveying and Facilities Management courses, paving the way for numerous graduates in Sri Lanka and globally, demonstrating a commitment to innovation and education.

Building Economics Students Society (BESS) is a renowned organization that foster a sense of empowerment and positive change for undergraduates, who come from financially underprivileged backgrounds. Sustaining a scholarship program, assist them in accomplishing their academic goals is a core objective of the existence of the Students' Society.

Sarasvi Gee Sisila, the most anticipated event of the University calendar is a classical musical sensation and the main fundraising event that supports all the welfare activities carried out by BESS. The music event happens annually with renowned veteran artists who enriched Sri Lankan music arena providing an unforgettable experience to all our participants and corporate sponsors.
