Building Economics Students' Society (BESS)

Welcome to the vibrant community of Building Economics Students' Society (BESS) at University of Moratuwa. BESS is a dynamic platform dedicated to providing effective and sustainable solutions for all academic and non-academic issues faced by the undergraduate students of Quantity Surveying and Facilities Management of all 4 levels. As of last year, with the detachment of Facilities Management as a new department, BESS continues to evolve to cater specifically to Quantity Surveying undergraduates hereinafter.

BESS stands as the cornerstone for students within the department fostering a sense of community through various welfare activities planned throughout the year. This society plays a crucial role in bridging the gap between lecturers and students creating a supportive environment that extends way beyond the academic realm. Notably, BESS actively engages with alumni, providing scholarships to undergraduates facing financial difficulties and contributing to positive change in underprivileged communities through various initiatives including scholarships. One of the BESS's remarkable endeavors is printing essential reference books for students of all four levels, alleviating the financial burden on students in light of the country's economic situation. BESS is a hub of positive impact, orchestrating various initiatives that go beyond the classroom.
