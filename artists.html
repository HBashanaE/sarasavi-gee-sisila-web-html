Artists

Visharada Nirosha Virajini

Nirosha Virajini is an award-winning artist who is a vocalist, a musician, and a music director with a career spanning over 23 years. She is a versatile singer who has sung songs in several languages such as Sinhalese, Tamil, Hindi, and Malayalam. She was awarded the Best Female Vocalist as a playback singer at the Presidential Awards and Sarasaviya Awards in 2000, 2002, and 2016.

Visharada T. M. Jayarathna

T.M. Jayaratne is a legendary musician in the Sri Lankan music industry who is well-known as a vocalist and a violinist. He first entered the Sri Lankan music industry as a playback singer with "Lahiru Dahasak" written by Sunil Ariyarathne and composed by Premasiri Khemadasa and he released his first cassette “Tharanga Volume - 01” in 1978. He received the Presidential Award of Sri Lanka in 1978, 1979, 1980, and 1987 and in 2006 he was awarded the Best Male Singer award at the Raigam Tele Award Festival.

Visharada Jagath Wickramasinghe

Jagath Wickramasinghe, a gifted Sri Lankan musician who has detoured from his Engineering career to pursue his passion as a musician. He has worked as a vocalist, instrumentalist, music producer, music director, and university lecturer as well. He is especially interested in promoting Sri Lankan music in foreign countries and has toured around 40 countries performing. “Ran Tharakawan” is his first solo song released which was composed by himself. He was awarded the Presidential Award in 1995 and the Best Young Music Director at the Sumathi Awards.

Visharada Karunarathna Diwulgane

Karunarathna Divulgane, a renowned vocalist in Sri Lanka who was born in Divulgane, Kurunegala. He took his name as Divulgane at the time when he was hired for the “Rajarata Sewaya” radio station as a singer. He also released his first album “Esata Asuwana Maime” which was a huge success.

Sanka Dineth

Sanka Dineth is a young sensational artist with a favor for a variety of musical genres such as soft rock and pop, Latin, and Indian music. He was also influenced by not only Sri Lankan musicians such as Kasun Kalhara but also Western icons such as Enrique Iglesias and Bryan Adams. He is a talented instrumentalist with skills in guitar, piano, violin, and flute and also he has qualifications; a diploma in vocal classic Bhathkhande Music.

Ridma Weerawardhana

Ridma Weerawardena is an admired young musician who has introduced a new dimension to the Sri Lankan music industry. He is a talented vocalist and a music composer in the Sri Lankan music industry. His song “Kuweni” became a success within a few days of launch. Furthermore, his talent is not only in singing but also in dancing.
