Event Mission

A pinnacle of BESS's annual calendar is the Sarasavi Gee Sisila, the largest classical music extravaganza at the University of Moratuwa. Sarasavi Gee Sisila has become a beacon of musical distinction with over 25 years of classical brilliance. After a hiatus due to the pandemic, it was reignited last year for its remarkable Silver Jubilee. This year, under the unwavering guidance of the Head of Department Dr. Mrs. Anuradha Waidyasekara and the senior Treasurer of BESS Dr. Mrs. Tharusha Ranadewa, Sarasavi Gee Sisila will roll out the red carpet to reverberate its classical brilliance for the 26th time at Sumanadasa Courtyard. Dr. Tharusha's enthusiastic endeavors have consistently benefited the department, reflecting her overwhelming passion for the betterment of the community.

Sarasavi Gee Sisila is a significant get-together for the department as an annual ignition of bonds between lecturers, students, past students and the community. This event is not just a musical extravaganza; it is a fundraising initiative that promotes unity, interactive skills and relationships within the department, providing a unique recreational balance to the students’ heavy academic pursuits.

A distinctive feature of Sarasavi Gee Sisila is its platform for young musical prodigies to perform alongside industry maestros, offering a valuable opportunity to enhance their talents. In essence, Sarasavi Gee Sisila is more than an event; it is an unparalleled musical elegance and a celebration of the harmonious spirit that defines the Department of Building Economics at University of Moratuwa.
