About us

Sarasvi Gee Sisila is a classical musical event that supports BESS' welfare activities. The Students' Society supports undergraduates from financially underprivileged backgrounds by maintaining scholarship programs. BESS represent students' interests, provide academic support, and offer career guidance in Quantity Surveying and Facilities Management. Moreover, charity projects are organized to benefit the community outside of the university to satisfy students' social obligations and help them develop into respectable members of society.
